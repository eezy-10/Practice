Case Study:
Ethereal Branding

Category

Identity & Visual Narrative

The Brief

Ethereal required a visual language that felt both ancestral and futuristic. The goal was to redefine the luxury wellness space, moving away from sterile minimalism toward a warmer, more tactile brand identity that celebrates organic textures and intentional living.

Our challenge was to bridge the gap between high-end digital precision and the raw imperfection of natural materials, ensuring a consistent experience from the mobile interface to the physical packaging.

The Process

We began by exploring the "soul" of the brand through physical medium experimentation. Hand-mixed pigments, film photography, and tactile paper stocks informed our digital decisions.
